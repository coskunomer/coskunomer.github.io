Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25.08.2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. How Users Log In

Users can access the app in one of the following ways: By signing in with Google, Twitter(X), Azure(Microsoft), or Apple (for iOS and macOS) using OAuth. They will always have the option to continue anonymously without creating an account.

2. What Data We Collect

We collect only minimal data necessary to provide a personalized and functional experience:

  • From OAuth providers (Google/Twitter/Azure): Name, Email address, Profile photo
  • From within the app: Language, country, and theme preferences (set by the user), Class progress, and quiz completion data

We do not collect or store sensitive personal data such as passwords, location history, or device identifiers.

3. Where Data Is Stored

All user data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted backend-as-a-service platform. We do not operate our own servers.

4. How We Use the Data

Your data is used only to:

  • Personalize your app experience (language, theme, etc.)
  • Track your class progress and quiz results
  • Identify your account when logging in via Google or Facebook

We do not use your data for advertising or analytics, and we do not share it with any third parties.

5. Who Can Access the Data

Only the app owner has access to user data stored in the database. No one else, including partners or service providers, has access to your personal or usage information.

6. Data Deletion

If you would like your data to be deleted, send a request to:

fluentechtr@gmail.com

Your data will be permanently deleted within 14 days.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, feel free to contact us at:
