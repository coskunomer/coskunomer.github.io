Welcome to the support page for Learn Russian! Here you can find helpful information about the app, troubleshooting tips, and how to contact us if you need further assistance.

About Learn Russian

Learn Russian is designed for all levels — beginners to advanced learners. It offers a comprehensive learning experience including:

  • Reading library with diverse texts
  • Listening exercises to improve comprehension
  • Vocabulary building tools
  • Grammar lessons and practice

Our goal is to help you learn Russian effectively and enjoyably at your own pace.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do I reset my progress?
Currently, you can reset progress by reinstalling the app. We’re working on adding an in-app reset feature.
The app is not working or crashes. What should I do?
Try restarting your device and updating the app to the latest version. If the problem persists, please contact us.
Can I use the app offline?
Some features require internet access, but many exercises and the reading library can be used offline once downloaded.
